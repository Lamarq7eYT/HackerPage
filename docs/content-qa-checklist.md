# Incident Content QA Checklist

Use this checklist before adding or publishing a new HackerPage incident.

## Source Review

- Confirm the incident date and affected organization.
- Link at least one primary or high quality secondary source.
- Separate confirmed facts from interpretation.
- Avoid copying long excerpts from sources.

## Experience Review

- Check mobile layout at narrow width.
- Confirm keyboard navigation reaches interactive elements.
- Confirm reduced motion mode keeps the story readable.
- Add descriptive alt text for visual evidence or screenshots.
- Keep lesson copy practical instead of sensational.

## Final Pass

Read the page once as a visitor and once as a maintainer. The visitor pass
should answer what happened and why it matters. The maintainer pass should
confirm that the content is sourced, accessible, and easy to update.
