# BreachRoom Scene Prompts

## Sala base

`Dark premium cyber investigation room, central desk in perspective, large futuristic monitor as hero object, subtle neon cyan and alert red, brushed black metal, reflective glass screen, floating dust, elegant cables, cinematic shadows, surreal but believable, expensive and editorial`

## Monitor idle

`Large premium monitor glowing softly in cyan, faint grid UI behind glass, subtle reflections, breathing emissive light, mysterious and expensive`

## Zoom

`Aggressive cinematic zoom into the monitor, slight overshoot, scanlight streaks, glass reflection warping, tension building`

## Boot

`Black screen, controlled glitch, scanlines, warning red pulses, loading bars, premium security lab mood, not arcade`

## Playback

`Monitor dominant composition, layered 3D depth, red data streaks, cyan UI haze, space for editorial overlays, documentary cyber thriller mood`
